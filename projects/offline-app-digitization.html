What was built

End-to-End Digitization Workflow

1

OCR-Based Data Extraction

Automated extraction from physical 12th-grade application forms using OCR pipelines, replacing manual data entry at scale.

2

Automated Data Cleansing

Standardisation, deduplication, and validation rules applied post-extraction to ensure CRM-ready data quality.

3

Privacy-Compliant Workflow Design

Designed handling and storage protocols meeting data privacy standards across bulk student data at 8L application volume.

4

CRM Integration for Presales

Piped cleaned, validated lead data into the CRM, giving Presales teams immediate, actionable access to offline applicants.

Impact

Quantitative

  • 50K → 8L applications: 16× YoY capacity scale-up
  • Minimal manual intervention across the entire pipeline
  • Presales team able to act on offline leads at scale, same day

Qualitative

  • Improved data accuracy and lead availability for Presales ops
  • Processing speed increased dramatically vs manual workflows
  • Data privacy compliance maintained at high volume
  • Operational efficiency gains across the digitization chain
Key Learning

Automation + CRM integration compounds at scale

Large-scale digitization combined with automation and CRM integration doesn't just improve one metric — it compounds across operational scalability, data reliability, and team productivity simultaneously. The biggest unlock was making offline data a first-class citizen in the CRM: once leads were flowing in at the same quality as digital leads, Presales could treat them identically.
