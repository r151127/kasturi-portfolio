Seamless CRM-Integrated Call Recording Solution for Presales Teams

Type: Presales Tooling | Tools Used: SalesTrail, TATA Tele, Salesforce, Floww.ai, CRM APIs

Project Objectives

  • To deploy a solution that records both inbound and outbound calls, stores them in the cloud with downloadable public URLs, and syncs recordings and metadata in real-time to Salesforce and Floww.ai.
  • This is aimed at eliminating manual effort, enhancing data visibility, and supporting AI-based performance auditing.

Outcome

A streamlined system was implemented that automatically captures and links call data to CRM interactions. The solution supports real-time access for managers and AI tools, aligning with operational and quality control requirements.

My Role

  • Managed procurement approvals, vendor onboarding, and ensured a successful roll-out aligned with stakeholder expectations.

Key Deliverables

  • Full compliance with call auditing and AI-analysis readiness.
