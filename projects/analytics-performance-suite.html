Comprehensive Analytics & Performance Intelligence Platform

Project Title:

Implementation of Multi-Product Analytics Dashboard Suite with Real-Time Performance Tracking

Project Objectives:

To develop and deploy a comprehensive analytics ecosystem spanning Academy, NIAT, and Intensive products, providing real-time insights into lead flows, CBA performance, incentive calculations, and operational efficiency. This initiative aimed to transform data-driven decision making across all business verticals while reducing manual reporting efforts and improving strategic visibility.

Outcome:

Successfully delivered 25+ specialized dashboards including CAC analysis, lead scoring, TAT tracking, incentives automation, and performance monitoring systems. Achieved significant time savings (14+ hours monthly for incentive calculations alone), improved data accuracy by 81%+, and enabled proactive management through real-time analytics across all product lines.

My Role:

Analytics Program Manager & Strategic Implementation Lead

My Responsibilities:

  • Led cross-functional analytics requirements gathering across Academy, NIAT, and Intensive product teams
  • Designed and implemented dashboard architecture using Looker Studio, Floww.ai, and custom database solutions
  • Managed vendor relationships and tool integrations for analytics platforms
  • Coordinated with CRM teams to ensure data consistency and accuracy across all reporting systems
  • Established KPIs and performance metrics framework for BDAs, BDMs, and CBAs
  • Oversaw the automation of incentive calculations, reducing manual effort and eliminating calculation errors
  • Implemented lead scoring models and conversion funnel analysis to optimize sales processes
  • Delivered training programs for business teams on dashboard usage and data interpretation

Deliverables:

  • 25+ operational dashboards covering lead management, performance tracking, and financial analytics
  • Automated incentive calculation system saving 14+ hours monthly with 100% accuracy improvement
  • Real-time TAT analysis dashboards reducing process bottlenecks across channels and regions
  • CAC and conversion funnel analytics improving marketing ROI visibility
  • Employee attrition analysis dashboard supporting HR strategic decisions
  • Comprehensive lead scoring and qualification tracking systems
  • 6-hour weekly time reduction for Operations Team through streamlined processes
  • Enhanced data-driven decision making across 3 major product verticals (Academy, NIAT, Intensive)
